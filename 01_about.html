ABOUT

At the beginning of my post-secondary study, I simply imaged that I would like to work within some kind of design fields like graphic design. Thus, I entered the School of Interactive Arts and Technology. Through working with different teams and playing different roles, I have explored my abilities and interests. Then, I landed at the place between engineer and design where I find a lot of fun. Finally, I realized that the web and game developments bring me even more fun and this is the direction I am passionate to continue in the future.

Contributed in various projects, I was usually in charge of low/high-fidelity prototype using different programming languages, application/software system design following object-oriented model and communicating with other designers to solve technical problems. I am comfortable with programming in C#, Java, JavaScript and HTML5. I also have experienced game developments using Unity 3D and created several interesting mobile App. In addition, I am constantly learning and practicing unfamiliar development tools such as Unreal Engine and WordPress.
