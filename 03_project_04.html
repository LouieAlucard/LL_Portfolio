Overview

Coursera is one of the biggest platforms which offers MOOCs (Massive Open Online Course). During my Co-op at UBC CTLT, I have been invited to the Coursera course design team and supported the team to construct the online courser "Useful Genetics".

The tasks I was responsible for were quiz/exam creation, lecture video editing and course material management.

Contribution

Lecture video and quiz assessment are two major teaching/learning methods within Coursera. After receiving lecture recording from the instructor, I am in charge of editing all lecture videos. In addition, for each video, based on requirements from the instructor, I was asked to embed short quizzes into the lecture video using Coursera tools. Asking questions during the lecture can help learners to reflect on the material to promote understanding and memorizing.
