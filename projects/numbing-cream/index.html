When to Use

Apply BioZkin Numbing cream to minimize the pain:

  • Usage of Needles
  • Waxing / Laser Hair Removal
  • IV insertion
  • Blood Tests / Blood Donation
  • Vaccination / Immunization
  • Cannulations
  • Micro-Dermabrasion
  • Laser Treatments
  • Tattooing / tattoo Removal

Fast Acting and Longer Numbing Times

Directions

Apply before injection, tattoo or laser surgery or as directed by physician or trained professional. Numbing time will vary according to procedure. Typical numbing time occurs in 30-45 minutes and can last for 3 hours depending upon the method of application. Dressing after application generally is not required.

Consult your doctor before using dressing along with application of the numbing cream. Maximum recommended dose for adults: 0.5 grams per dose. (approximately 0.75 inches or 1.8 cm in length of a ribbon of cream dispensed from the tube). For geriatrics, pediatric, and patients with other underlying conditions (eg. kidney and liver impaired) consult the doctor for dosing adjustments.

It is best to apply the cream 30-40 minutes before the procedure. A minimum 10-15 minutes.

Power of 3 numbing agents:

  • Benzocaine 20%
  • Lidocaine 6%
  • Tetracaine 4%

Caution

For external use on intact and healthy skin only. Avoid applying to open wounds, near or inside the eyes, burns, rashes, (including diaper rash). Discontinue use and consult a doctor promptly if conditions worsen or if symptoms persist for more than 7 days. Do not use if allergic to benzocaine, lidocaine, tetracaine or any other ‘caine’ type of anesthetics.

Storage

  • Store between 15-30oC.
  • Protect from light and freezing.
  • Keep out of reach of children.

FAQ

How do the three active ingredients work?

Each active ingredient numb the skin at different rate and time. Numbing effect of benzocaine is fast but only numbs for a short time; numbing effect of lidocaine is slower than benzocaine but lasts longer than benzocaine; finally numbing effect of tetracaine starts later than benzocaine and lidocaine, but numbing effect last the longest. This combination results in overall quick and long lasting numbing effect from this product.

Why is bioevolve numbing cream oil base?

Oil based cream adheres to the skin better and last longer on skin.

Are there any harmful preservatives added?

Absolutely not. We do not add any harmful or potentially questionable ingredients (e.g. parabens) to our product as they can harm your skin and body.

The colour of the cream is yellowish brown, is this normal?

Yes it is. Again, we do not use any preservatives or cream whiteners in our product. So that is the natural colour of the finished product. Over time this cream will brown even more as the oil is oxidizing. This is normal and will not harm the product.

What is the pH of your product?

Our product tested in the 5.5 pH range.
