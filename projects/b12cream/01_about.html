"BioZkin Quality and Standard"

All BioZkin products are formulated with strictly controlled standard and have to be completely safe and clean.

  • No SLS, parabens, DEA, MEA, TEA, phthalates, MCI/MI, sulphates, PEG & gluten
  • No artificial colors, fragrances, flavours, fillers or other potential allergens
  • No animal testing
  • Free of color additives, artificial flavor, and other potential allergens
  • GMO free
  • All plant extracts and essential oils must be certified organic whenever possible
  • All plant oils must be cold pressed, not heat distilled - which kills the therapeutic properties of plant oils
  • All essential oils used must be steam-distilled and not extracted with propylene glycol - which kills the effect of the oils
  • All ingredients must be proven to be beneficial to the hair, skin, body, or total health
  • Clinically proven scientific data

All of our BioZkin products are made with the total health of the human in mind. Stringently screened by a team of experts, BioZkin products are clean, safe and simply effective.
